# Book Marketing Video Script Template

Use this template for creating marketing videos for individual books or the complete series.

---

## Video Type: {{VIDEO_TYPE}}

Options:
- **Book Trailer** -- Cinematic 60-90 second teaser
- **Author Introduction** -- 2-3 minute author speaking about the series
- **Chapter Reading** -- 5-10 minute reading of a compelling excerpt
- **Series Overview** -- 3-5 minute walkthrough of all books
- **Behind the Scenes** -- 3-5 minute look at the writing process
