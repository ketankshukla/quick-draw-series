COLOR CONTINUITY:
The front cover, spine, and back cover must feel like one continuous image or design. Colors, lighting, and atmospheric effects should flow seamlessly across the wrap.

ANTI-PAINTERLY DIRECTIVE:
Photorealistic quality throughout. Real textures, real lighting, cinematic rendering. NOT illustration, NOT painting.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Resolution: 300 DPI minimum (print quality)
- Color space: CMYK for print
- File format: PDF (preferred) or high-quality TIFF
- Bleed: 0.125 inches on all edges
